# Final Genesis Account and Data Deletion

Final Genesis uses Google sign-in and stores online gameplay data through Supabase.

To request deletion of your account and associated data:

1. Send an email to yopsadida@gmail.com from the same Google account used in the app.
2. Use the subject `Final Genesis account deletion`.
3. Include your in-app display name if it is different from your email profile name.

After the request is verified, we will delete or anonymize account profile data, selected character data, ranking records, matchmaking data, private room data, match history, and online presence data associated with the account.

Some technical records may be retained temporarily when required for security, abuse prevention, backup recovery, legal compliance, or service integrity. Backup copies are removed through the normal backup rotation process.

You can also request deletion of gameplay data without deleting the Google account itself by using the same email process and describing which data you want removed.
